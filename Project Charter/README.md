The Project Charter outlines the plan to design and launch a 100% biodegradable, reusable water bottle within an eight-month timeline. It defines the project’s purpose, scope, objectives, major deliverables, budget, stakeholders, and key risks. The charter formally authorizes the project, ensuring alignment with the company’s sustainability goals and providing clear direction for successful execution.
